# **App Name**: Farsh Bazaar

## Core Features:

- Vendor Directory: A searchable directory where فرش فروشان (carpet vendors) from around the world can create profiles, showcasing their expertise, location, and specialties.
- Social Networking: Enable carpet vendors to connect, communicate, and build relationships, facilitating knowledge exchange and potential collaborations.
- Virtual Showrooms: Premium users can create their own online 'rooms' to display their فرش (carpets), offering detailed descriptions, high-quality images, and contact information.
- Investment & Partnership Platform: Create a platform where فرش vendors and businesses related to carpet-making can seek and offer support for related activities.
- Global Trade Hub: Establish the app as a central place for تجار (merchants) to connect, discover opportunities, and conduct business, thereby helping carpet trade across the world.
- Cultural Promotion Tool: Use AI as a tool to generate stories related to hand-woven فرش, increasing appreciation for the artistry, history, and cultural relevance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, rich red (#8B0000), reminiscent of traditional Persian carpets.
- Background color: A very light beige (#F5F5DC), providing a neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: A muted gold (#B8860B) to highlight premium features and calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif with an elegant, high-end feel. Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif suitable for body text
- Use icons inspired by Persian carpet motifs and patterns.
- Clean, grid-based layout to showcase the فرش (carpets) and vendor profiles effectively.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user engagement.
